Welcome Pets R Us, your local neighborhood pet store located in Tucson, Arizona. We can help you with all your pets needs. We understand that your pet is a part of the family and when you shop with us you can feel confident knowing that we wouldn’t sell anything that we wouldn’t give to our own pets. At our store you will find a great selection of pet food, treats, toys and accessories, all at low prices. In our store you will also find friendly Pet Experts trained in pet care and nutrition. Thinking about getting a pet? Not only do we sell pet supplies but we also have a wide selection of pets available for adoption. All our available pets come from rescue organizations and shelters around the state, so you know that when you adopt from us you are saving a life. We pride ourselves on being pet experts and we want to use our knowledge to help you with any of your pets needs.
